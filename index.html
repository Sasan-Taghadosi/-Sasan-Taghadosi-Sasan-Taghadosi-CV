Interests

My passion for mathematics started in grade one! I remember my father used to practice addition and subtraction with me, I was able to answer his questions quickly and correctly. Since then, I have always been fascinated by mathematics and statistics. There was never any doubt that I would pursue my career in a quantitative field.

During my MBA program, I decided to dive deeper into quantitative finance. Therefore, I chose to pursue Risk and Finance as my specialization. So I took all the relevant courses and always got the highest marks in my classes.

On the other side, I realized that big data and new AI models are the must-know knowledge for quant people, especially in financial services, so I spent a lot of time learning the new concepts, and their application in financial services, along with the tools and programming languages to implement them.

In my point of view, anyone who wants to work with data, must first make sure that the data that he or she is going to work on is accurate or at least high quality. Because if the quality of data is not good, then the model, no matter how good it is, will generate low-quality output leading to wrong decisions. Thus, after graduation, I started my career as a data quality analyst and acquired excellent skills in analyzing the quality of data.

I am interested in working in quantitative fields such as risk analysis and measurement in financial services, where I can bring my finance knowledge along with my data and programming skills to the table to design and build data reporting tools, risk monitoring dashboards, and predictive and prescriptive models to help make better well-informed data-driven decisions.
