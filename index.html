Interests

My passion for mathematics ignited in grade one! I fondly recall my father engaging me in practice sessions of addition and subtraction, where I swiftly and accurately answered his questions. From that moment on, I became captivated by the world of mathematics and statistics. It left no room for doubt that I would embark on a career in a quantitative field.

During my MBA program, I made a conscious decision to delve further into the realm of quantitative finance. As a result, I opted to specialize in Risk and Finance. This choice led me to enroll in all the pertinent courses, where I consistently excelled and achieved top grades among my peers.

On the other side, I recognized the indispensability of comprehending big data and emerging AI models for individuals in quantitative roles, particularly within the financial services sector. Consequently, I dedicated a substantial amount of time to acquire a deep understanding of these novel concepts, as well as their practical application in financial services. Additionally, I diligently familiarized myself with the tools and programming languages necessary for their implementation.

From my perspective, it is paramount for anyone venturing into the realm of data to prioritize ensuring the accuracy and, at the very least, the high quality of the data they are working with. The quality of the data plays a pivotal role since even the most exceptional model would yield subpar output if fed with low-quality data, potentially resulting in misguided decisions. Consequently, upon completing my studies, I deliberately embarked on my professional journey as a data quality analyst, honing my abilities to meticulously scrutinize and assess the quality of data. Through this role, I acquired exemplary skills in analyzing data quality.

I possess a keen interest in pursuing a career within quantitative fields, specifically in risk analysis and measurement within the financial services industry. My ambition is to leverage my knowledge in finance, coupled with my adeptness in data analysis and programming, to contribute to the design and development of cutting-edge data reporting tools, risk monitoring dashboards, and predictive and prescriptive models. By doing so, I aim to facilitate more informed, data-driven decision-making processes that ultimately lead to better outcomes.
